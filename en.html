Description

💡With a lot of features:
  • 📝 Scan QR Codes and Barcodes: Use your device's camera to quickly scan QR codes and barcodes in seconds.
  • 📁 Available formats: QRCode, Code 39, Code 93, Code 128, EAN-8, EAN-13, UPC-A, UPC-E, ITF, Data Matrix, AZTec, PDF 417, Codabar.
  • 📷 Scan QR code Images from your photo library.
  • 🎨 Generate QR codes and barcodes: Create your own QR codes and barcodes with custom designs and information.
  • 📚 Manage Your History: Save and organize your scan history and creation history for easy access.
  • 📱 Share with Ease: Share scanned and generated QR codes across your favorite social platforms with just a few taps.
  • 🎨 Customize QR codes according to your preferences.
  • 📂 Batch Scanning: Scan multiple QR codes and barcodes at once with our batch scanning feature.
  • 📊 Export Data: to CSV, Text, or Image formats for easy analysis and sharing.
  • 🌎 Multilingual Support: QRBay supports up to 18 languages, including English, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Chinese, Thai, Korean, Japanese, French, German, Spanish, Polish, Turkish, Russian, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Arabic, Hindi.
