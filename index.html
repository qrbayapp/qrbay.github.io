Description

With a lot of features:
  • Scan QR Codes and Barcodes using your device's camera.
  • Available formats: QRCode, Code 39, Code 93, Code 128, EAN-8, EAN-13, UPC-A, UPC-E, ITF, Data Matrix, AZTec, PDF 417, Codabar.
  • Scan QR code images from your photo library.
  • Generate QR codes and barcodes.
  • Save scan history and creation history of QR codes and barcodes.
  • Easily share scanned and generated QR codes across your favorite social platforms with just a few taps.
  • Customize QR codes according to your preferences.
  • Batch scanning capability.
  • Export data to CSV, text, or image formats.
  • Multilingual support for up to 10 languages, including English, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Chinese, Thai, Korean, Japanese, French, German, Spanish, and more expansions.
